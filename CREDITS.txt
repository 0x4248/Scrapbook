includes 0x4248.dev, 0x4248 Scrapbook, 4248 Music, and 4248 Media. It is 
formatted similar to the linux CREDITS file. 

The fields are Library name (L), Name of person or company who made the library 
(N), Copyright or license (C), Website (W), Description (D), Address (A)
